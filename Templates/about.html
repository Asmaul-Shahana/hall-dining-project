About our website

In a university, thousands of students admitted every year. Therefore, in every residential university allotted their required hall seat per student. The students of these universities can eat their meal from hall and also outside. But the outside meals are unhygienic and costly, so they prefer to eat from hall. Sometimes they unable to collect their coupon in exact time because they have classes, or some other problem like weekend. For this they suffer a lot to collect their coupon.

Student can book coupon both online and offline. For online booking, student must create an account. Using this account, they can view menu and then book coupon. They can also view the upcoming events in this system. Students can also cancel their coupon in due time both online and offline. In this system, students can pay the coupon bill by any online systems like Bkash, Nagad, Rocket. Any student can pay bill in person during office hours. After completing payment, they will get a confirmation mail and then can download the coupon. In this system, there are some stuffs who serves in the dining, daily groceries shopping, cooking etc.

The main objective of this system software is to build up a platform where the user can communicate with the system via internet. The system is always updated by the admin of the system. The objectives of the system are:

  1. Improvement of the coupon booking system
  2. Can search for menu list
  3. Also, can search for upcoming events
  4. User can select the menu from the require list
  5. Staff can also see his/her work schedule when he/she has to serve, cook or shopping
  6. Dining manager waits for the Hall provost command they are connected each other
  7. For every event a budget is fixed
