ABOUT US

We bring you the delicious, healthy and comfort meals with fresh ingredients for an amazing dining experience.



The students of these universities can eat their meal from hall and also outside. But the outside meals are unhygienic and costly, so they prefer to eat from hall. Sometimes they unable to collect their coupon in exact time because they have classes, or some other problem like weekend. For this they suffer a lot to collect their coupon.

Student can book coupon both online and offline. For online booking, student must create an account. Using this account, they can view menu and then book coupon. They can also view the upcoming events in this system. Students can also cancel their coupon in due time both online and offline. In this system, students can pay the coupon bill by any online systems like Bkash, Nagad, Rocket. Any student can pay bill in person during office hours. After completing payment, they will get a confirmation mail and then can download the coupon.
