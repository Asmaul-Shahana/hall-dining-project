Frequently Asked Questions

Coupons are available only for future meals upto next three days and upcoming events. Regular meals cannot be booked at the day of the meal. Coupons will not be accepted after the day of the meal is passed.

You pay both online and manually. For paying manually, you must pay before the meal and coupon must be verified by the dining manager.

To cancel a coupon, you must request the dining manager in person within five hours. After that the refund will not be applicable.

Our system allows only the students of running acdemic years. For any special renewal, you have to send an application to the hall provost for approval. After dining manager extends the access, you can book coupons again.

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

  • For any query or help, contact with the authority.
  • You can meet our dining manager during the office our for any problem related to payment, renewal, refund or other issues.
  • For any server issues, let us know as soon as possible.
  • Our service is closed during the official day offs of the hall.
